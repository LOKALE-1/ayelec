About The Convergence

United in Diversity.
Driven by Purpose.

AYELEC is a premier continental ecosystem and convergence platform that intentionally connects youth, entrepreneurs, investors, business leaders, policymakers, governments, development partners, academia, industry experts, faith-based organisations, civil society, the private sector, and leaders across all sectors to advance entrepreneurship, innovation, enterprise development, investment, and inclusive economic growth throughout Africa.

AYELEC South Africa 2026 is envisioned as a continental platform where youth, entrepreneurs, enterprises, investors, governments, academia, development partners, faith-based organisations, civil society, the private sector, and leaders from diverse sectors converge to exchange knowledge, forge strategic partnerships, unlock investment opportunities, and accelerate sustainable socio-economic transformation across Africa. Guided by the belief that Africa's legacy is intentionally crafted by multi-sectoral stakeholders, AYELEC cultivates meaningful collaborations that create lasting impact for present and future generations.

  • Engage directly with a network of entrepreneurs, SMEs, innovators, enterprises, investors, and decision-makers from diverse sectors across Africa.
  • Showcase investment strategies, business solutions, products, services, technologies, and organisational value propositions to a continental audience.
  • Build strategic relationships with local, regional, and international businesses, governments, investors, funders, and development partners.
  • Identify high-potential opportunities for investment, mentorship, acquisitions, market expansion, enterprise development, and strategic collaboration.
  • Contribute to strengthening Africa's entrepreneurial, investment, innovation, and leadership ecosystem by fostering meaningful partnerships between capital providers, enterprises, institutions, and communities.
  • Participate in high-level dialogue and networking with business leaders, policymakers, government officials, academics, investors, development partners, faith leaders, industry experts, and other influential stakeholders committed to Africa's transformation.
  • Collaborate across sectors to co-create practical solutions that promote ethical leadership, sustainable development, job creation, shared prosperity, and a lasting continental legacy

Leadership Strategy

Tshepiso Malema

Entrepreneur · Agritech Innovator

Founder and Group CEO of Tech4Africa Group and the founder of Flockify, empowering farmers through technology.

Tshepiso Malema is a South African entrepreneur, poultry farmer, and technology innovator. He is the Founder and Group CEO of Tech4Africa Group and the founder of Flockify, an agritech platform focused on empowering farmers through technology and innovation.

Recognised for his work in entrepreneurship and innovation, Tshepiso was named among the Mail & Guardian Top 200 Young South Africans and received recognition for BRICS Best AgriTech Solutions. He was also recently selected as a BRICS delegate to China for an international training exchange programme on Science and Technology Governance and Cross-Border Innovation.

Through his work, Tshepiso is passionate about building African solutions at the intersection of agriculture, technology, and youth empowerment, while inspiring the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs.

Agritech Innovation

Noluthando Gama

E-commerce Expert · Innovator

Founder of Scan2Sell App and Ecom Lifestyle, helping entrepreneurs build and scale online businesses.

Noluthando Gama is the Founder of Scan2Sell App and Ecom Lifestyle, with over seven years of experience in e-commerce, online marketplaces, and dropshipping.

She specializes in helping entrepreneurs build and scale online businesses by providing mentorship, marketplace solutions, and technology that simplifies product sourcing and selling. Through her work, she has assisted thousands of aspiring entrepreneurs across South Africa in starting and growing their e-commerce businesses.

As a young entrepreneur and innovator, Noluthando is passionate about empowering Africa’s youth through entrepreneurship, digital transformation, and sustainable business opportunities. She is committed to inspiring entrepreneurs with the knowledge and tools needed to create lasting economic impact across the continent.
